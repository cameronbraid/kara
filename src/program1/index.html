The Project

The Nessuno Project was developed in 1940 during World War II as a spy agency for the Allied forces. Since then, it has become a top secret British special operations unit. The NP (as it is known now) often works in conjunction with MI5 and MI6.

Only select people even know about this agency - apart from the agents - including but not limited to the Prime Minister, the chief executives of MI5 and MI6 and the police commissioner. The general public have no knowledge of this agency, and for the most part, they have remained under the radar of enemy intelligence units.
